Instructions

  • Click on the game you want to play. For example if you clicked on “3. Park”, you will see

  • Once it opens the page teach the words and phrases as needed.
  • Click on Start to start the game
  • It will start a 30 seconds timer
  • Ask the students to watch and remember the pictures and the positions
  • Once the timer stops, all the images will be hidden
  • Pick a player by asking for a volunteer or choosing a student
  • Now click on Play

  • It will show a “?”.
  • Ask the player to guess the picture in the box shown with “?”
  • After they tell the name click on Show; it will show the picture
  • Depending on the students’ age and Tamil skill you can ask them to tell more about the picture before showing the picture. You can ask prompting questions like:
  • எத்தனை மரம்?
  • எத்தனை பறவை?
  • பறவை என்ன நிறம்?
  • அவங்க என்ன பண்றாங்க?
  • …

Enjoy!
